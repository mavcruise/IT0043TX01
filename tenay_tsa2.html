TRAVEL ADVISORIES

Cebu road closure on May 25

Attention: Passengers flying to and from Cebu

Road closures will be implemented across Cebu to make way for a sports event happening on May 25, Saturday.

We highly encourage you to allocate extra time going to the airport to ensure that you will make it in time for your flight. Passengers may use the 1st and 2nd bridge to and from Mactan Cebu International Airport.

Plan to arrive at the airport at least 3 hours before your domestic flight; or atleast 4 hours before your international flight.

Please check your updated flight status before heading to the airport by visiting www.philippineairlines.com and clicking the Flight tab in the PAL website.

You may also check-in online via the PAL website or your mobile app within 24 hours up to one hour before your flight.

Thank you and we wish you all a pleasant journey.
